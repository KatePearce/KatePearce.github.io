Kate Pearce

My name is Kate Pearce, and I'm an aspiring computer scientist working in civic and educational technology.

My research interests mostly lie in computational linguistics (check out my work on the NLP page!), and I've been merging this interest with applications in misinformation detection at the Text Analysis Lab at the University of Arkansas and political watchdogging at Code for Democracy.

Additionally, my experiences as a tutor at schoolhouse.world have led me to pursue projects in educational technology; my proposal for an intelligent tutoring system was a finalist in the 2021 MIT THINK competition, and I'm also currently working with Edia to develop dynamic content modules.

Most of all, though, I strive to learn all I can in order to create a more equitable, empathetic, and compassionate world.
