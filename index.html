Kate Pearce

Hello! My name is Kate Pearce, and I am a technology student at Ignite Professional Studies. I am highly passionate about creating and utilizing technology in order to improve the world around us.

My research interests lie in machine learning and computer science, especially natural language processing. I recently worked on research to develop a better model for extractive question answering, and I am involved with the Text Analysis Lab at the University of Arkansas.

I am also a volunteer tutor at schoolhouse.world. My experiences as a volunteer tutor inspired me to do work in the field of intelligent tutoring systems, and my proposal for an intelligent tutoring system to predict student learning preferences was one of the finalist projects for the 2021 MIT THINK competition.

I am very excited to learn more about technology and help build a better world.
