Volunteering and Teaching

Schoolhouse.world

Schoolhouse.world is a nonprofit founded by Sal Khan, creator of Khan Academy, that aims to provide free, virtual tutoring to anyone, anywhere.

I've been active on the platform for almost a year and a half, and during my time, I've taught about 350 sessions to learners in 49 countries and received over 1600 positive ratings. Additionally, I also co-lead the tutor mentorship team, which oversees the ongoing development of tutors' skills, and serve as an auditor and onboarding buddy.

Join Schoolhouse here!

I work as a Code Sensei at CodeNinjas, where I teach computer science fundamentals to elementary and middle school students. Check back later for more updates!
